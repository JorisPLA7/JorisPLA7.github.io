Public VR Room

Date: 2018 - 2019
Project pitch (2 min)
Read the PDF (french)

I am leading this project within the University of Grenoble since December 2018. I finally raised 5000€ and a dedicated room for the project! This project involves the installation of state-of-the-art virtual reality equipment in a room at the Joseph Fourier University Library. (Featuring Both Oculus Rift S and Valve Index) Access is free of charge and upon reservation. Such a room allow students to become aware of the potential of virtual reality, both through playful discovery and by helping learning in their field of study. This equipment can also serve as a working support for study projects. The purpose of this project is to share one of my passions and to make Grenoble students actors rather than spectators of VR innovation and future society transformations.
